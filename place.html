A forest??? In Manhattan???

That's right! At the veeeery northern tip of Manhattan lies Inwood Hill park, a glimpse into what the natural landscape of the island looked like before all the skyscrapers and traffic. Caves and old forts dot the hills which give the park its name, as well as several hiking trails and the odd mushroom . Apparently if you're lucky, you might be able to spot a bald eagle if you visit!

I went there

For my thesis project, I needed to take some reference photos of an overgrown, forested place. Inwood Hill park was the easiest such place for me to get to. I went with a friend and we did some life drawing there... until it got too cold and dark and we had to go home.
