TITLE WEEEEEE!!!! The save button....

The save button is an icon many are familiar with, but few know the historical context of. It's a simplified representation of a 3.5 inch floppy disk, a form of storage that is considered outdated. Updated alternatives such as a downward arrow exist, but they are more commonly associated with the act of downloading, rather than specifically saving a file.
